Lake Park Resort –
Your Serene Escape in Sapanca Sakarya


Nestled in the heart of Sapanca Sakarya, Lake Park Resort is a hidden gem that offers an exclusive retreat from the hustle and bustle of daily life. Our resort boasts 12 elegantly furnished villas, each complete with its own private swimming pool. Each villa is also equipped with 4 rooms and two showers, ensuring a tranquil and intimate experience for all our guests.

Luxury Meets Nature
At Lake Park Resort, we believe in providing an experience that combines the comfort of luxury with the serenity of nature. Our villas are designed to blend seamlessly with the surrounding landscape, offering breathtaking views and a peaceful environment. Whether you’re looking to relax by the pool or explore the natural beauty of Sapanca, our resort is the perfect base for all your adventures.

Amenities Tailored to Your Comfort
Each villa at LPR is equipped with modern amenities and services to cater to your every need. From daily housekeeping to room service, we ensure that your stay is as comfortable and hassle-free as possible. Reach Us at Lake Park Resort

Lake Park Resort is your perfect getaway nestled in the heart of nature. We’re conveniently located in Rüstempasa, Adiyaman Sk No 33/1, Sapanca, Sakarya, Turkey.

By Car 🚗

Our resort is well-connected by roads. You can use any popular GPS service to navigate to our location. Just type in “Lake Park Resort, Sapanca” and you’ll be on your way!

By Train 🚆

The nearest railway station is Arifiye, which is just 6 km away from the resort. From there, you can take a taxi or use local transportation services to reach us.

By Air ✈

The closest airport is Sabiha Gökçen International Airport - SAW. It’s about a 90-minute drive from the resort. Taxis and shuttle services are available from the airport.
